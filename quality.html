Quality Assurance

Discover our rigorous quality control process that guarantees excellence in every batch.

Our Unwavering Commitment to Quality

At Sunshine Polymers, quality is the foundation of our 30+ year legacy. We are committed to achieving complete customer satisfaction by providing products that consistently meet and exceed expectations.

Our quality management system provides a robust framework that governs every step of our process at our Pune facility, from the initial sourcing of plastic scrap to the final inspection and dispatch of finished granules.

Advanced In-Process Testing

Our in-house laboratory is at the heart of our quality control. We utilize modern testing equipment to meticulously analyze every batch, ensuring all physical and chemical properties align with required specifications.

  • Melt Flow Index (MFI) Testing: We measure the exact viscosity and flow rate of our granules, which is crucial for predicting polymer behavior during processing.
  • Density & Tensile Strength: We verify the material's strength, durability, and weight to ensure it meets the demands of its intended application.
  • Color Consistency: Our tests ensure perfect color matching for your specific requirements, batch after batch.

Final Inspection and Client Satisfaction

Before any product leaves our facility, it undergoes a final, comprehensive inspection. We believe that this last check is critical to guaranteeing that our clients receive nothing but the best quality we are known for.

  • Lot-wise Verification: Every lot is checked for visual defects, granule uniformity, and contamination.
  • Transparency and Trust: We maintain detailed records of our quality checks and can provide necessary data to our clients, ensuring full transparency.
  • Guaranteed Satisfaction: Our three decades of experience are built on a simple promise: to deliver reliable, high-quality material that helps your business succeed.
