Our Products

A look at our high-grade recycled plastic granules and raw materials for every industrial need.

High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Granules

HDPE is renowned for its exceptional strength-to-density ratio. Our recycled HDPE granules are resistant to chemicals and ideal for robust products like pipes, jerry cans, industrial drums, and crates.

  • Outstanding Impact Strength & Durability
  • Excellent Resistance to Chemicals & Corrosion
  • Ideal For: Water Pipes, Jerry Cans, Crates, Buckets

Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) Granules

LDPE is a soft, flexible, and lightweight material. Our premium reprocessed LDPE granules are perfect for manufacturing clear or colored plastic bags, food wrap films, liners, and flexible squeeze bottles.

  • High Toughness and Flexibility
  • Excellent Moisture Resistance
  • Ideal For: Shopping Bags, Squeeze Bottles, Liners, Film

Polypropylene (PP) Granules

Polypropylene is a highly versatile thermoplastic known for its excellent chemical resistance, high heat tolerance, and rigidity. We supply high-quality reprocessed PP granules suitable for injection molding.

  • High Heat and Chemical Resistance
  • Lightweight yet Strong and Durable
  • Ideal For: Automotive Parts, Furniture, Luggage, Containers

Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) Granules

LLDPE stands out for its superior flexibility and high puncture resistance, making it the preferred material for high-performance films. We provide top-grade granules for stretch wrap, agricultural films, and heavy-duty sacks.

  • High Flexibility and Elongation
  • Exceptional Puncture and Tear Resistance
  • Ideal For: Stretch Films, Agricultural Liners, Flexible Lids

Sorted Plastic Scrap

We source and process high-quality post-industrial and post-consumer plastic scrap. This scrap is meticulously sorted, cleaned, and shredded to serve as a valuable raw material for reprocessing and a circular economy.

  • Eco-Friendly and Sustainable Raw Material
  • Carefully Sorted by Polymer Type and Color
  • Ideal for Reprocessing into Recycled Granules
